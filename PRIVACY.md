# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2024

## 1. Introduction

Pollacle ("Platform", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

## 2. Data We Collect

### When You Create a Fundraiser
- Title, story, goal amount, category
- Optional: email address
- IP address, browser info

### When You Complete Surveys
- Survey responses (via CPX Research)
- Session ID, completion confirmation
- IP address, device info
- Reward amount

### Automatically Collected
- IP address (rate limiting, analytics)
- Browser type, OS version
- Pages visited, time spent
- Cookies (for session management)

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use data to:
- Operate the Platform
- Process fundraisers and surveys
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Improve user experience
- Comply with law

## 6. Data Retention

- **Fundraiser data:** Kept while active; deleted on request
- **Survey completions:** 90 days for auditing
- **Log data:** 30 days for security
- **CPX data:** Per CPX Research policy

## 7. Security

We implement:
- HTTPS encryption (SSL/TLS)
- Input validation (Zod schemas)
- Rate limiting
- Database access controls
- Regular audits

**No system is 100% secure.** We cannot guarantee absolute protection.

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

We use:
- **Session cookies:** Keep you logged in
- **Analytics cookies:** From Vercel
- **Third-party cookies:** From CPX Research

You can disable cookies in browser settings.
